WHAT IS BACKLIT AND FRONTLIT?

BACKLITBacklit-designed mirror has light emanate from behind the mirror towards the entire space, providing ambient lighting and aesthetic to the room. The ambient lightning illuminates the back fo the wall, crating a textural wall with layers of depth especially on a tiled wall.

FRONTLITA Frontlit-designed mirror has forward facing lighting which is brighter for user as it allows light to come forward to the face. Frontlit is an very effective as it reduces the overhead shadows typically from downlights and therefore, it suits best for grooming.
